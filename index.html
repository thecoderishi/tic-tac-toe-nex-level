Basic Things To Know Before Playing This Game

1. Player-1 is X and Player-2 is O.

2. Both players have 6 gobbler, and each gobblers have size from 1 to 6.

3. You can start with any piece of your gobbler.

4. Put a new gobbler on the board, on an empty space or over smaller gobbler.

5. You can gabble up your own pieces.

6. Line up 3 of your gobblers in a row to win!


NOTE : you can not change your gobbler after grabbing it.
